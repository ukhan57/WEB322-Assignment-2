Top 5 Best Anime Series Of All Time.

Posted on October 1, 2022 by Umar Khan
Best anime series according to IGN Best anime series according to IMDb

Anime refers to animation originating from Japan, characterized by a unique art style that emphasizes artistic detail and camera effect. Anime include television series, films, OVAs, and web series. This is list of top 5 greatest anime I have ever watched, some may include critical acclaimed classics, top rated series, and international sensations.

WARNING: Some anime descriptions may include SPOILERS!

#1 Death Note

Death Note is a Japanese television drama anime series based on the manga series of the same name by Tsugumi Ohba and Takeshi Obata. The show is centered around Light Yagami - a promising highschooler and aspiring detective with a strong sense of "justice".

"It the most amazing anime I've ever seen. It was a thrilling and amazing experience to watch this anime series. The ending was fitting to the characters, especially Yagami light. At the same time, it was sad to see the end of the main protagonist dying at such young age by having no one by his side. Another interesting and heart throbbing character was the L, his skills as the detective was topnotch. The lesson of the series is well conveyed, no matter how much injustice is in the society we can't take the law in our hands."

#2 Attack On Titan

Attack on Titan, also known as Shingeki no Kyojin in Japanese, created by Hajime Isayama, is one of the most popular Japanese anime series to be released. It was first watched in Japan during 2013 and was dubbed in English to be watched in America in 2014. It is best known for its emotional moments, apocalyptic setting, and its dramatic story line, and action packed scenes.

"It the most amazing anime I've ever seen. It was a thrilling and amazing experience to watch this anime series. The ending was fitting to the characters, especially Yagami light.
